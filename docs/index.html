FAQ

Quick answers.

Does it work in every app?

Yes. Slapppy is system-level — it works in any app on your Mac: browsers, editors, Slack, email clients, Notion, terminals, everything.

Does it need an internet connection?

No. All recognition runs entirely on your Mac. Your patterns and text snippets never leave your device.

How do I trigger a pattern?

Hold ⌥ (Option) from any app, then tap your rhythm or draw your gesture on the trackpad. Slapppy fires the mapped action instantly.

Is there a Windows version?

Not yet — Slapppy uses macOS-exclusive trackpad APIs that don't exist on other platforms. MacBook only for now.

Is it a one-time purchase?

Yes — €9.99 once, yours forever. No subscription, no renewal. You also get all future updates for free.

How long does setup take?

Under 60 seconds. Open Slapppy from the menu bar, tap "Record", perform your rhythm or gesture, assign a text snippet, shortcut, apps, or URLs to open — done.
