Why Slapppy

Your trackpad can do
a lot more than scroll.

Apple built a remarkable piece of hardware under your fingertips. Slapppy unlocks it as a personal command layer — no keyboard, no mouse, no context switch.

🥁

Tap rhythms — like Morse code

Hold ⌥ and tap a rhythm on your trackpad. Short-long, double-quick, three slow taps — any pattern you can remember. Slapppy's fuzzy recognizer matches it even when you're not perfect.

✏️

Draw gestures — like signing your name

Hold ⌥ and move the cursor in a shape — a Z, a check mark, a circle. Slapppy uses the $1 Unistroke algorithm (the same tech used in gesture-based UIs) to recognize your drawing and fire the action.

💬

Output anything, instantly

Map each pattern or gesture to a text snippet (types into any app like a keyboard) or a keyboard shortcut. Your email, a meeting link, a code snippet, a muted mic — triggered in under a second.
