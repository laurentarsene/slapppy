EU consumer rights

If you are a consumer in the European Union, you have a statutory right of withdrawal of 14 days from the date of purchase for digital goods, in accordance with Directive 2011/83/EU — provided you have not yet downloaded or accessed the software.

If you have already downloaded Slapppy, the right of withdrawal may no longer apply under EU law. However, as a courtesy, we honour our 14-day refund policy regardless.
